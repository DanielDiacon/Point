Motivation is a way to quickly achieve a goal, based on a psychophysiological process that directs a person's behavior, contributes to his activity and performance. It represents a set of motives.
According to the Explanatory Dictionary of V. Dahl, motive from French means a motive, a reason. To motivate means to give arguments that justify an action or prove the necessity of an action.
